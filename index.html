BED & BREAKFAST

A Bed & Breakfast is a lodging option where guests stay in private rooms within a family home or small inn and are provided with breakfast in the morning.

Escape to our sustainable B&B surrounded by nature. Enjoy breakfast on the sunny patio or stroll through the forest.

Our bed and breakfast has cozy and comfortable indoor spaces. Each room is uniquely decorated with elegant furnishings, creating an upscale feel

Our bed and breakfast also offer its own recreational amenities such as swimming pools, hot tubs, and sports courts.

Our smoking rooms are located in designated areas of the hotel, and are equipped with all the amenities you would expect from our other guest rooms.

Our rooms are checked and certified by B&B Ireland, a quality assurance program that ensures your stay with us meets the highest standards of cleanliness, safety, and comfort.

We love pets at our hotel! We offer pet-friendly accommodations so that you and your furry friend can enjoy a comfortable and safe stay with us. We provide amenities such as pet beds and food dishes, and have designated areas for walking your pet.
